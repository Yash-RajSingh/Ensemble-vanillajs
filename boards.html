Here's how to use Ensemble

  • Here at Ensemble, we refer to workspaces as boards.
  • To add workspaces, just fill in the required fields in the box on the left and hit add
  • Create as many boards as you need, there's no limit.
  • To open a workspace, just click on the desired workspace and you'll be refirected to your workspace
  • Add lists to plan out your tasks and monitor them
  • Invite people to work with and work together
